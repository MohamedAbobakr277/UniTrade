## 2026-04-12 - Missing ARIA Labels on Core Navigation and Actions\n**Learning:** Icon-only buttons for critical actions like favorites, notifications, and social links frequently lacked `aria-label` attributes, which significantly impacts screen reader users' ability to navigate and interact with the primary marketplace functions.\n**Action:** Always add descriptive `aria-label` attributes to any `<IconButton>` components, especially those that toggle states (like favorites) or trigger navigation (like "Sell an item").
